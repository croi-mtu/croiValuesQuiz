Welcome to Croí

About This Questionnaire

Croí – Your Life, Your Career is a values-based reflective education initiative developed at Munster Technological University (MTU) to help you identify your personal core values and align them with your academic, professional, and life goals.

This questionnaire will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. You'll be asked to rate 57 statements on a scale from 1 (not like me at all) to 6 (very much like me).

Understanding Your Results

After completing the questionnaire, you'll receive a personalized value profile showing your top values. Click on any value in your results to learn more about what it means.

For a complete guide to all 19 values, download the Croí Values Thesaurus (PDF).

Academic Attribution

This questionnaire is based on the Portrait Values Questionnaire-Revised (PVQ-RR) developed by Professor Shalom H. Schwartz. The PVQ-RR measures 19 basic human values derived from Schwartz's refined theory of basic values.

Schwartz, S. H., et al. (2012). Refining the theory of basic individual values. Journal of Personality and Social Psychology, 103(4), 663-688.
